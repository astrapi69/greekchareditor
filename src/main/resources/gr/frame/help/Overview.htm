Overview

The tool was designed to convert latin characters in greek characters. It converts latin characters to greek HTML-entities or "ISO-8859-7"-characters or "greek-unicode"-characters. The appropriate characters are outputed in the appropriate editors when the "Transform"-Buttons are pushed. The idea was created when we was looking for a tool in the internet that offer this functionality. Cause at that time we not found such a tool we decided to programm. The tool is intended for Webdesigner who need to put greek characters in the HTML-file.
